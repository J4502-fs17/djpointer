About Me

My name is originally Deja but I've been giving the name DJ because of my energetic personality and strong interest in music.My first and middle initials also spell out D.J. The next thing to note is I love to explore different cultures and music. My YouTube Channel is all about me listening to different music from Korean,Hindu, and Indian music industries and seeing if I like the music that is the topic for that week. I started my YouTube originally for a school blog in 2016 but since, it has become a hobby that I enjoy doing and that my subscribers love seeing me do. When I started college, I was a big advocate for stepping outside my comfort zone and submersing into different cultures. I got really involvecd with Chinese culture. I started eating a bunch of Chinese food, mastering the use of Chopsticks, and listening to a lolt of C-Pop and K-pop music.

I'm a senior at the school of journalism studying multimedia producing. I'm very passionate about hip hop music and culture and I love to report on things happening in the music industry. I want to use digital platforms to engage people to talk about music!

During my college career, I was given the name DJ by my peers and professors because of my energetic spirit and awareness of music and the culture that it embodies. I soon discovered a love for video and digital storytelling. I would love to create a bigger outlet for people to engage with content using multiple media platforms. I want to do this by creating new ideas for audience engagement and digital storytelling. The things I like about digital storytelling is that though information can be reached quicker by and audience but you can get more creative with the way content is displayed.
